Raven

Informations

Raven (civilian name Rachel Roth ) is one of the main characters of the Teen Titans series. She is a human-demon hybrid, originating from the parallel dimension of Azarath, and is one of five founding members of the Teen Titans.

ABILITIES

  • Capable Combatant : Though not the most physically talented of the quintet, Raven has been seen as a fairly good hand-to-hand combatant, although she only occasionally uses this skill during battle.
  • Multilingualism : Raven claims to know English, German, Latin, Romanian, Ancient Sumerian, and Sanskrit.
  • Occultism : Raven has considerable supernatural knowledge, keeping a library of numerous spell books in her room on the occult, and gained further sorcerous knowledge from Malchior.
